Fairness¶

MIMUW XAI WINTER 2022/2023¶

Task 1¶

Demographic parity:

$\frac{P( \hat{Y} | A = B) }{P( \hat{Y}| A = R)} = \frac{0.65}{0.5} = 1.3$

Equal opportunity:

$\frac{P( \hat{Y} | A = B, Y = 1) }{P( \hat{Y} | A = R, Y = 1)} = \frac{0.75}{0.5} = 1.5$

Predictive rate parity:

$\frac{P( Y | A = B, \hat{Y}= 1) }{P( Y | A = R, \hat{Y} = 1)} = \frac{\frac{60}{65}}{0.5} = \frac{120}{65} \approx 1.85$
